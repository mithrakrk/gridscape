# Level Design

## Level Progression
- Grid density increases with each level.
- Early levels have no obstacles; later levels introduce simple floating blockers.
- Every level must be mathematically solvable.

## Starter Levels & Artworks
The first 5 levels reveal famous-art-inspired targets:
1. **Level 1**: Michelangelo-inspired composition (The Creation of Adam).
2. **Level 2**: Leonardo da Vinci-inspired composition (Mona Lisa).
3. **Level 3**: Raphael-inspired composition (School of Athens).
4. **Level 4**: Banksy-inspired stencil mural composition.
5. **Level 5**: Original/Abstract composition introducing blockers.

## Reveal System
- The target wall grid maps to regions of the hidden artwork.
- Painted cells reveal the corresponding region.
- Unpainted cells remain darkened or muted.
- Level selection should tease the artwork style.
