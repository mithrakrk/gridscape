# Skill: Level Design

## Instructions
- Read `docs/level-design.md` for the progression strategy.
- Ensure every level is mathematically solvable.
- Map the artwork assets appropriately to the grid density of the level.
- When placing obstacles, test the trajectory paths to ensure they don't completely break the solvability of the level.
- Start with zero obstacles for early levels and scale difficulty through grid density and required formula complexity.
